Sophie sighed, trying to process everything. “So what do I do now?”

Martha gave her a reassuring smile. “You should go out and see the world for yourself, Sophie. You don't have to stay in the hat shop forever.”

Sophie looked down at her hands. “But what if I'm not meant for adventure?”

Martha took her hand gently. “You won't know until you try.”
